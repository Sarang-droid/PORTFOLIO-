About Me

I'm a college dropout from Pune driven by a mission to bridge the communication gap between industries and students. Without formal business training, I taught myself AI, finance, and systems design to build Skillexa.

My goal is to empower students with skills that match industry needs, ensuring they land dream jobs. Skillexa fosters collaboration and drives employability, aiming to impact 225,000 students by 2028.

Skillexa: My Vision

Only 45.9% of engineering graduates in India are employable. Skillexa addresses this with an AI-driven platform matching students to projects, live at skillexa.in.

Using Real-Time Job Insights (RTJI) and Canopy Cash Flow Management (CCFM), our beta engages thousands, aiming for 225,000 users and $5M in seed funding by 2026.

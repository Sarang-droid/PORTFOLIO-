About Me

I'm a student from Pune driven by a mission to bridge the communication gap between industries and students. Without formal business training, I taught myself AI, finance, and systems design to build Skillexa.

My goal is to empower students with skills that match industry needs, ensuring they land dream jobs. Skillexa fosters collaboration and drives employability, aiming to impact 225,000 students by 2028.

Skillexa: Built from Frustration, Driven by Hope

Every year, millions of students step out of college with dreams — and find doors closed. Not because they lack talent, but because the system never prepared them for the world they were entering.

I watched it happen around me. I lived it. Good students. Great potential. But no real-world projects, no clear guidance, no bridge to the skills companies actually need.

Left on their own, students turn to courses and certificates, hoping to fill the gap. But with thousands of "must-have" courses flooding the market, confusion takes over. Students pay not just their college fees — but spend even more on endless courses, chasing a future that still feels uncertain.

Skillexa was my answer.

On Skillexa, students don't waste time guessing what matters — they take action. Here's how it works:

  • 🔹 Login to access a real-world project marketplace across multiple industries.
  • 🔹 Browse projects and select the ones that match your interests and career goals.
  • 🔹 Get curated resources alongside each project — no more confusion, only focused learning.
  • 🔹 Complete and submit your project — a real, skill-based accomplishment, not just a certificate.
  • 🔹 Earn points and feedback within 1 hour based on the quality of your submission.
  • 🔹 Showcase your work instantly on your profile — companies see proof of your skills, not just words on a resume.

Powered by Real-Time Job Insights (RTJI) and Canopy Cash Flow Management (CCFM), Skillexa evolves as fast as the world does — ensuring our users are always ahead of the curve, not chasing it.

This is bigger than education. It's a movement to shift the power back to the learners. A future where skills matter more than degrees, and opportunities are earned, not bought.

By 2028, Skillexa will empower 225,000 students, redefine employability, and reshape how the world hires. And this is just the beginning.

Building the Backbone Before Scaling

Startups don't usually fail because of bad ideas — they fail because they lack strong systems, processes, and adaptability. When I began building Skillexa, I made a conscious decision: before chasing massive growth, I would build the internal backbone first.

That's why systems like Canopy Cash Flow Management (CCFM), Real-Time Job Insights (RTJI), the Tree and Branch skill model, and our evolving Canopy AI were created from the beginning — to ensure Skillexa is financially resilient, market-adaptive, and built to scale intelligently, not recklessly.

At Skillexa, we believe: Build slow. Build strong. Build forever.

Canopy Cash Flow Management (CCFM)

CCFM ensures Skillexa's sustainable scaling by managing liquidity in real time. Built with FastAPI and React, it tracks our 6.6-day Cash Conversion Cycle and 51-day runway, saving over 10 hours per week.

With automated alerts and a crisis-proof reserve system, CCFM will evolve alongside Canopy AI to predict future cash flow patterns by 2030 — preparing us for long-term scaling and our $5M seed funding journey.
